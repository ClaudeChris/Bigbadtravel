Big Bad Travel — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Big Bad Travel (“the app”) is built to protect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app uses and how.

We do not collect your data

Big Bad Travel has no servers, no account system, no analytics, and no advertising. The developer cannot see your trips or any information you enter.

Your trip data

The trips, stops, options, notes, and packing lists you create are stored on your device and, if you enable iCloud, synced privately through your own Apple iCloud account. Only you can access this data — the developer has no access to it.

Location

If you allow it, the app uses your device’s location solely to show your current position on the map while you plan. Your location stays on your device and is never collected, stored by us, or transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Place photos

To show a photo for a destination, the app requests publicly available images from Wikipedia using the place name (for example, “Jackson, Wyoming”). No personal information is sent, and these requests are not linked to you.

Maps

Maps and place search are provided by Apple Maps and are subject to Apple’s privacy policy.

Children

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page.
